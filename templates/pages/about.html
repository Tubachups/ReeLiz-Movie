    HISTORY

    ReeLiz Cinema was founded in 2015 with a vision to transform the local movie-going experience through innovation and accessibility. What began as a small independent screening hub for local films has evolved into a modern entertainment brand that embraces technology and creativity. Over the years, ReeLiz has continuously adapted to changing trends in film and digital media, offering seamless booking systems, immersive viewing experiences, and a strong connection to Filipino film culture.

  • Founder
